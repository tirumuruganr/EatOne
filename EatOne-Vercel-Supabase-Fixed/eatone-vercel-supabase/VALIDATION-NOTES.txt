Added checkout validation:
- Name accepts letters, spaces, apostrophe, hyphen and period only.
- Phone accepts digits only, maximum 10 digits, and validates Indian mobile numbers starting 6-9.
- Pincode accepts digits only, exactly 6 digits, first digit non-zero.
- Shipping is calculated only after a syntactically valid 6-digit pincode.
- Order button remains disabled until customer details and shipping are valid.

Note: This validates Indian PIN format and maps shipping by the region logic already in App.jsx.
For authoritative serviceability of every Indian PIN, connect a courier/pincode database/API.
